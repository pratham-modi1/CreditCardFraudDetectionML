SHOULD I DO ALL 25 PREPROCESSING STEPS BEFORE EDA? No.
Only do basic preprocessing first:
- Data inspection
- Handle missing values
- Remove duplicates
- Basic transformations (like scaling, log if obvious)

Then move to EDA.
EDA will tell you:
- What features matter
- What needs more transformation
- What to keep or drop

Key Rule:
EDA → then further preprocessing (not before)
Conclusion:
Current preprocessing is enough. Move to EDA.
